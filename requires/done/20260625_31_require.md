## [Medium][eventbus][health] Add health and observability signals for retry backlog, DLQ lag, and consumer failure rate

### Summary
The current health model reports binary liveness, but not delivery-semantics degradation.

### Problem
The system can be “alive” while retry backlog grows, DLQ-eligible events remain live, or consumer failures spike.
Those conditions are not visible in the current health model.

### Scope
- /health
- runtime observability
- delivery metrics
- operational documentation

### Task
- add health indicators for:
  - retry backlog
  - DLQ-eligible-but-not-isolated events
  - consumer failure rate
  - timeout rate
  - DLQ promotion lag
- document the meaning of those degraded states
- add operator guidance for alerting and remediation

### Expected Result
Operators can detect delivery failure degradation before it turns into silent message loss or persistent replay confusion.

### Acceptance Criteria
- /health or related observability exposes delivery degradation signals
- retry/DLQ backlog is observable
- consumer failure rate is observable
- the new operational signals are documented
