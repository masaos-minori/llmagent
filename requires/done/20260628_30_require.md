# Review and remove ingest-specific DTOs and exceptions

## Priority
Medium

## Labels
agent, services, cleanup, dead-code, ingest

## Dependencies
- Remove or isolate `IngestWorkflowService`

## Background
`IngestWorkflowService` uses ingest-specific support types such as `IngestStage`, `IngestStageError`, and `IngestOutcome`. These may become unused after `/ingest` removal.

## Task
Remove ingest-specific DTOs and exceptions if they are no longer used.

## Implementation Requirements
Search for:
- `IngestStageError`
- `IngestStage`
- `IngestOutcome`

If these are only used by the removed ingest workflow:
- Remove them from their defining modules.
- Remove imports and tests.
- Update any `__all__` exports if present.

If they are used elsewhere:
- Keep them and document the remaining owner.

## Acceptance Criteria
- No unused ingest-specific DTOs or exceptions remain.
- Lint/type checks pass.
- Non-ingest services are unaffected.

## Source Context
`ingest_workflow.py` depends on `IngestStage`, `IngestStageError`, and `IngestOutcome` for stage-aware error handling and results.
