## [Low][ops][eventbus] Add push-mode operational guidance for queue sizing, slow-consumer policy, and resync strategy

### Summary
A push-based live-delivery model introduces new runtime behavior that operators need to understand.

### Problem
Without operational guidance, push-mode issues such as queue buildup and slow consumers will be difficult to diagnose and tune.

### Scope
- 06_eventbus_05_configuration_deploy_and_operations.md
- push-mode runbooks
- operational tuning guidance

### Task
- document queue sizing strategy
- document slow-consumer handling policy
- document fallback/resync behavior
- explain when replay is used after broker disruption
- add troubleshooting guidance for push delivery

### Expected Result
Operations can deploy and maintain the push-based Event Bus without needing to infer behavior from source code.

### Acceptance Criteria
- push-mode operational guidance exists
- slow-consumer policy is documented
- queue sizing guidance is documented
- replay/resync behavior is documented
- troubleshooting guidance references the new runtime model
