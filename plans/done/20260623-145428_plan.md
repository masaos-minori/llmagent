## Analysis: Why FIFO Is Correct for SemanticCache

SemanticCache uses linear scan (`O(n)` cosine similarity over all entries) ‚Äî it does not track access frequency or recency of hits. LRU would require tracking last-access time per entry, which adds complexity without benefit for a bounded 100-entry in-memory scan cache. FIFO is the right policy.
