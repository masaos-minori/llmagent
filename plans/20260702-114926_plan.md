**Key finding â€” two propagation layers:**

- **Layer 1 (RerankStage.run):** `RagRerankError` propagates up to `_run_stage()`.
- **Layer 2 (_run_stage):** `_run_stage()` catches `RuntimeError` (superclass of
  `RagRerankError`), records `StageResult.status="failure"`, logs a warning, and
  **does not re-raise**. The pipeline continues; `ctx.reranked` stays `[]`.

The operations doc incorrectly says "Fall back to RRF order" â€” the actual behavior
is "failure recorded in StageResult; pipeline continues with empty reranked list."

**Blast radius:** Docs-only and test-only changes. No runtime behavior change.
